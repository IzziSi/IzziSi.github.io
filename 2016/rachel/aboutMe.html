Rachel Heiser

Hair Stylist

Rachel is a graduate of Aveda Institue of Jacksonville. Specializing in blondes and fashion colors as well as precision cutting and bridal styling.

She has been trained in Aveda, Redken, Pravanna and Kenra color. Always on top of the trends, she has taken classes lead by Sam Villa and Ruth Roche of RARE Salon in New York and has been to small classes taught by Wendy Balanger of Influence Salon in Calgary and Justin Thomas with Hattori Hanzo shears.

When not in the salon Rachel chooses to spend time with her family. Whether it's swim team, ballet or P.T.A. she can typically be found with her kids, Harper and Eleanor.
